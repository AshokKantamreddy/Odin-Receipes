Lasagna

Description

lasagna can be made with various combinations of ricotta or mozzarella cheese, tomato sauce, meats (such as ground beef, pork or chicken), and vegetables (such as spinach, zucchini, olives, mushrooms), and the dish is typically flavoured with wine, garlic, onion, and oregano.

Ingredients
  • Meat
  • Onion & Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices & Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • cheeses
  • Eggs
Steps to Prepare
  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover the foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
